Editing Things

Adding and changing text in your Delectus documents is as simple as clicking the text you want to change and typing the new text. Each piece of text in the Main List Area is editable. Simple click the text and type your changes.

The text of in a column's label in the Delectus window is not editable, but you can change a column's label by clicking the label, then choosing Rename Column from the List menu.
