Tips

  1. Hide and Show the Buttons: Click the gray lozenge button at the top right corner of the Delectus window to hide or show the Add and Delete buttons.

  2. Hide and Show the Trash: Click the Trash Button to show or hide deleted items. If shown, deleted items appear in red. When deleted items are shown, the Trash Button displays a red label that says "Deleted items are red".

    Note: If you show deleted items and there aren't any, then no items will be red, but the Trash Button will still show the red message.

  3. Open Recent Lists: Use the "Open Recent" item in the "File" menu to open Delectus files that you have used recently.

  4. Rearrange Columns: You can drag a column to a new place in the window. Simply grab the column's label and drag it to the new place yu want it to be. Delectus will remember the order you choose.

    You can also change the width of a column by dragging its right edge. Grab the right edge of the column's label and drag it to resize the column. Once again, Delectus will remember the size you choose.
